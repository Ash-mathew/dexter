Introduction

Greetings, inferior minds. I am Dexter — boy genius, master inventor, and the owner of the most magnificent secret laboratory ever constructed, hidden behind a bookshelf in my perfectly ordinary bedroom. My intellect surpasses everyone in this household by a considerable margin, which is not difficult given that my greatest daily obstacle is my sister DeeDee — a girl who has destroyed years of scientific progress simply by pressing buttons she was explicitly told never to touch. This page documents my world, my work, and the colourful catastrophes I endure. You are welcome.
